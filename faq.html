Frequently Asked Questions

What is this weather app?

Our weather app provides accurate and real-time weather information sourced directly from the National Weather Service API. It helps you plan your day by offering detailed weather forecasts and updates.

How do I search for weather in a specific location?

You can search for weather by entering the name of your city or using the location feature to get weather updates for your current location.

How often is the weather information updated?

The weather data is updated in real-time using information from the National Weather Service API, ensuring you always have the most accurate and up-to-date forecasts.

Can I receive weather alerts?

Yes, you can customize weather alerts for specific conditions like severe weather warnings, temperature changes, and more. Simply go to the settings and select your preferences.

What devices are supported?

Our weather app is accessible on all modern web browsers, and is optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.

Is there a cost to use the weather app?

Our weather app is completely free to use. We provide this service to ensure everyone has access to reliable weather information.

How do I report a bug or provide feedback?

If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please visit our Contact Us page and fill out the form. We value your feedback and aim to improve our app continuously.
